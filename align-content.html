Align-Content

This aligns a flex container's lines within when there is extra space in the cross-axis. similar to how Justify-contentaligns individual items within the main axis.

Note:This property has no effect when there is only one line of flex-items

Lets talk about available values.

  • flex-start
  • flex-end
  • center
  • strech
  • space-between
  • space-around
